Promoting Diversity and Inclusion in the Company

Our core value of "Responsibility" ensures that we treat our employees, customers, and business partners fairly. Therefore, we are committed to providing all colleagues and applicants with equal and fair opportunities, regardless of factors such as age, social or biological gender, sexual orientation, ethnic or national origin, religion or belief, disability, marital or partnership status, with part-time or temporary employment.

Our goal is to build a diverse and inclusive workforce that values diversity in all its forms, positively impacting the business and well-being, and driving our competitive advantage.
